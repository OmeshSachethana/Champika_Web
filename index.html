Our Process

A Simplified and Optimized Process

From beginning to end, our comprehensive recruitment process involves active engagement by our team with both candidates and clients to ensure the right match.

01

Job Order Review & Business Insight

The principal sends a job order, then reviews and analyzes to gain insight into the business environment, company culture, compensation, and other relevant factors.

02

SLBFE Approval & Legal Compliance

We obtain the approval of the SLBFE (Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment) for the particular job order, to ensure the processes are legal and up to the government requirements.

03

Targeted Job Promotion Across Media

We leverage our broad network of newspapers, TV channels, and social media platforms to promote the job opening to potential candidates, highlighting the job description.

04

Candidate Screening & Preliminary Interviews

We screen and evaluate candidate resumes based on the job requirements, client specifications, and our quality standards, including reference checks. Shortlisted applicants then undergo preliminary interviews by our trained professionals to identify the most suitable candidates.

05

Job Offer & Documentation Preparation

After reviewing the finalized candidates, the client extends a job offer. We collaborate with the selected candidate to prepare and organize the necessary documentation for overseas employment.

06

Pre-Departure Processing & Final Clearance

We guide candidates through pre-departure procedures, including medical exams, visa processing, and certificate attestation. The team handles SLBFE (Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment) registration and final approvals, assists with travel arrangements, and oversees the candidate's departure.

Top Work Destinations

Bridging Sri Lankan talent with global opportunities

Kuwait

Kuwait is a long-standing destination for Sri Lankan domestic workers, drivers, hospitality professionals, and engineers, offering tax-free income and structured work environments.

Bahrain

Bahrain continues to attract Sri Lankan talent in sectors such as healthcare, hospitality, retail, and mechanical trades — known for its supportive work culture and growing job market.

Qatar

The Qatar job market is robust, driven by significant investments in infrastructure, energy, and tourism. It features a high demand for skilled professionals in sectors like construction, engineering, education, and finance.

UAE

The UAE job market is dynamic and diverse, driven by a strong economy and a strategic location. It offers opportunities across various sectors, including finance, technology, hospitality, healthcare, and construction.

Saudi Arabia

The Saudi Arabia job market in construction is booming, fueled by the government's Vision 2030 initiative, which aims to diversify the economy and enhance infrastructure.

Turkey

Turkey continues to offer job placements for professionals in tourism, food and beverage, healthcare, and construction — attracting Sri Lankan talent with competitive packages and cultural harmony.

Romania

Romania has emerged as a key destination for Sri Lankan workers, offering skilled and semi-skilled job opportunities in sectors like construction, hospitality, manufacturing, and agriculture.

Russia

As one of the world's largest economies, Russia offers exciting opportunities for electricians, drivers, hospitality workers, and caregivers from Sri Lanka.
